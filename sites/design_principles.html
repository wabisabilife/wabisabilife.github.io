DESIGN PRINCIPLES

The elements that make up this type of design are: rough finish, simplicity and minimalism, asymmetry, imbalance, flat and neutral colors, textures, organic influences, disharmony. Designers who inject a bit of wabi-sabi into their design give it more life, even though we're talking about digital media. Wabi-sabi beauty provides a visual balance to projects because it is basically the opposite of a design style based on a polished digital screen.
